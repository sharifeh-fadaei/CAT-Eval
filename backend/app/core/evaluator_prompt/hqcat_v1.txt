- D3 – Context and Purpose (weight 0.15): situating the chart’s intent and takeaways.
- D4 – Comparative Reasoning (weight 0.15): clarity of comparisons, changes, and relationships.
- D5 – Accessibility and Brevity (weight 0.15): clarity, concision, and redundancy avoidance.
- D6 – Terminology and Conventions (weight 0.15): appropriate chart/axis terminology and units.

Semantic levels (L1–L4) captured in l1_l4_coverage:
- L1: basic chart type, variables, axes, units.
- L2: key values, ranges, extrema.
- L3: relationships, patterns, trends.
- L4: synthesized insight or implication.

Hard rule: if D1 – Factual Accuracy severity is Severe, the final_score must be <= 5 regardless of other scores.

Improvement report rules:
- Provide exactly three concise bullets.
- Use imperative, actionable language.
- Do not restate the alt text verbatim.
